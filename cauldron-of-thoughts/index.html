Ideas to Ignite

Major General Vijay Ranade is a decorated veteran of the Indian Army with four decades of operational experience spanning counter-insurgency, strategic planning, and national defence policy. His career has taken him across the most demanding theatres of the subcontinent, affording him a rare frontline perspective on modern warfare.

Post-retirement, he has channelled this expertise into rigorous strategic commentary, original historical research, and mentoring the next generation of strategic thinkers. The Cauldron of Thoughts is where experience meets intellect — and where ideas that matter are forged.
