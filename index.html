Vijay Ranade

Strategic Analyst · Military Historian · Author

Major General Vijay Ranade is a decorated veteran of the Indian Army with decades of operational experience spanning counter-insurgency, strategic planning, and defence policy. His career has taken him across the most demanding theatres of the subcontinent, giving him a rare firsthand perspective on modern warfare.

Post-retirement, he has channelled his expertise into rigorous strategic commentary, historical research, and mentoring the next generation of strategic thinkers. The Cauldron of Thoughts is his platform to distil that experience into ideas that matter.
